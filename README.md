# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard (2003–2005)

An interactive sales dashboard built in Google Sheets analyzing 2,763 orders across 19 countries.  
This is **Project 1** of my Data Analyst portfolio.

---

## 🔍 What I Analyzed

- 2,763 unique orders from 2003 to 2005
- £10,032,628 in total revenue
- 7 product lines across 19 countries
- Average order value of £3,553

---

## 💡 Key Insights

| Insight | Finding |
|---|---|
| 🏆 Top product line | Classic Cars — dominated all other categories |
| 🌍 #1 market | USA — contributed the largest share of revenue |
| 📈 Revenue trend | Clear seasonal peaks in Q4 every year |
| 🥈 #2 market | France — second highest revenue by country |
